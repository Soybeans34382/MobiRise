Our Features

FocusFlow removes decision fatigue, eliminates wasted time, and helps you build consistent focus habits that compound over time
Smart Timer

Traditional Pomodoro timers are rigid. FocusFlow isn’t.
Our Smart Timer learns from your real focus patterns—when you’re sharp, when you drift, and when you need a break. Session lengths and rest periods automatically adjust over time, helping you maintain energy without burnout. You stay focused longer without forcing yourself into an artificial schedule.

 Prioritization

Never wonder “what should I work on next?” again.
FocusFlow’s AI analyzes deadlines, task complexity, urgency, and long-term impact to recommend the most effective next action. Whether you’re juggling school, work, or side projects, the system keeps you moving forward—one high-leverage task at a time.

 Analytics

Your focus leaves patterns—FocusFlow reveals them.
Track productivity streaks, peak focus hours, distraction triggers, and long-term trends. Turn raw data into actionable insights that help you refine your workflow, improve consistency, and build habits that actually stick.
